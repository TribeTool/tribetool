    How To Apply Runway

    The Runway Startup Postdoc Program is part business school, part research institution, and part startup incubator. Based at the Jacobs Technion-Cornell Institute, Runway ushers recent PhDs in digital technology fields through a paradigm shift — from an academic mindset to an entrepreneurial outlook.

       
    How To Apply Spinouts

    To date, students have created 18 startup businesses that together employ 32 people. Many of those new businesses helped along by Cornell Tech’s Startup Awards, which provide funding and other support to student teams who want to spin out their Startup Studio projects after graduation.
